Interactive · embedded in landing page

The brand name does the work. "Club" reframes repair as something you join, not something you do alone. The conspiratorial wink is built into the premise — you're in on this, with everyone else who's in on it.

The visual world is a confident chromatic four — cobalt, red-orange, mustard yellow, lavender pink — held together by a calm cream and charcoal base. No color is "the brand color"; the system flexes piece by piece, the way a real club's materials would.

Six deliverables in one cohesive language: wordmark system, event poster, membership postcard, three-piece IG tile series, web landing page, and an embedded two-question interactive quiz called Repair Diagnostics. Built as a parallel project — the system holds at the same scope without burning my real ideas for an applied campaign brief.

AboutNora Vertus

I'm a designer working in identity, campaigns, and editorial. I treasure hunt — at thrift stores, in archives, in old type catalogues. The pieces people pass on are almost always the ones with the most to say.

My eye comes partly from a Haitian household, where degaje — resourcefulness as artistry — is a quiet form of pride. That lens shapes what I notice and what I make.

I use AI tools openly as part of my production process. The voice is mine; the production is faster.

Currently: a recent public relations graduate, freelancing while studying for TEAS. Open to new work.
